Tutorial: Measures of Center
Understanding Mean, Median, and Mode

These three measures tell us where the "center" of our data is, but each gives us different information.

Mean (Average)

What: Add all values, divide by count

Use: When data is fairly normal

Affected by: Outliers (extreme values)

Median (Middle)

What: Middle value when ordered

Use: When data has outliers

Resistant to: Extreme values

Mode (Most Frequent)

What: Value appearing most often

Use: For categorical data

Note: Can have no mode or multiple modes

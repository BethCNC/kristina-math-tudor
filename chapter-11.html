Median (Middle Value)

The middle value when data is sorted. If there are two middle values, take their average.

Example 1 (odd count): 78, 85, 88, 90, 92
Median = 88 (middle value)

Example 2 (even count): 78, 85, 88, 90
Median = (85 + 88) / 2 = 86.5

Mode (Most Common)

The value that appears most frequently. A dataset can have no mode, one mode, or multiple modes.

Example: 5, 7, 7, 9, 12, 7, 15
Mode = 7 (appears 3 times)

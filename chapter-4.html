Key Formulas

Percentage Formula

Part = (Percent/100) × Whole

To find a percentage of a number, multiply by the decimal form of the percentage

Example: 25% of 80 = 0.25 × 80 = 20

Proportion Formula

a/b = c/d

Cross multiply: a × d = b × c

Example: 3/4 = x/12 → 3×12 = 4×x → x = 9

Practice Problems

Problem 1: Percentage

A shirt costs $40. If it's on sale for 20% off, what is the sale price?

Solution:

Discount = 20% of $40 = 0.20 × $40 = $8

Sale price = $40 - $8 = $32

Problem 2: Proportion

If 3 apples cost $1.50, how much do 8 apples cost?

Solution:

Set up proportion: 3/1.50 = 8/x

Cross multiply: 3x = 1.50 × 8 = 12

Solve: x = 12/3 = $4.00

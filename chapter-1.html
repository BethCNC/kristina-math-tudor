üìö Key Concepts

üîç Deductive Reasoning

Definition

Reasoning from general principles to specific conclusions. If the premises are true, the conclusion must be true.

Example

General Rule: All students must attend class to pass.

Specific Case: Kristina is a student.

Conclusion: Therefore, Kristina must attend class to pass.

üîÑ Inductive Reasoning

Definition

Reasoning from specific observations to general conclusions. The conclusion is probable but not guaranteed.

Example

Observations: It rained Monday, Tuesday, and Wednesday this week.

Pattern: It has rained every day so far.

Conclusion: It will probably rain tomorrow too.

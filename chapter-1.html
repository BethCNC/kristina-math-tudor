1.1

Problem Solving Strategies

Learn systematic approaches to mathematical problems

The Four-Step Problem Solving Process

1

Understand the Problem

Read carefully, identify what you're looking for, and determine what information you have.

2

Devise a Plan

Choose a strategy: draw a diagram, make a table, look for patterns, or work backwards.

3

Carry Out the Plan

Execute your strategy step by step. Be patient and persistent.

4

Look Back

Check your answer. Does it make sense? Could you solve it differently?

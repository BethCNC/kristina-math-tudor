ADHD-Friendly Study Strategies

Before the Test

24 Hours Before:

  • • Review formula sheet
  • • Practice 3-5 problems from each chapter
  • • Get 8+ hours of sleep
  • • Prepare calculator and materials

Test Day:

  • • Eat a good breakfast
  • • Arrive 10 minutes early
  • • Use bathroom before test starts
  • • Read all instructions carefully

During the Test

Test-Taking Strategy:

  • • Scan all questions first
  • • Start with easiest problems
  • • Show all work clearly
  • • Check answers if time allows

Time Management:

  • • Budget 2-3 minutes per problem
  • • Skip hard problems, come back later
  • • Use last 10 minutes to review
  • • Don't leave any blank answers

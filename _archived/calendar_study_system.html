📐 Quick Formula Reference

💰 Personal Finance (Chapter 6)

Simple Interest: I = Prt
Compound Interest: A = P(1 + r/n)^(nt)
Continuous: A = Pe^(rt)
APY: ((1 + r/n)^n - 1) × 100%

P=principal, r=rate (decimal), t=time, n=compounds per year

📏 Conversions (Chapter 7)

1 inch = 2.54 cm
1 foot ≈ 0.305 m
1 mile ≈ 1.61 km
1 pound ≈ 0.454 kg
F = 9C/5 + 32
C = 5(F-32)/9

📊 Statistics (Chapter 11)

Z-score: z = (x - μ) / σ
Sample z: z = (x - x̄) / s

μ=population mean, σ=population std dev
x̄=sample mean, s=sample std dev

🗳️ Apportionment (Chapter 13)

Standard Divisor:
SD = total pop / total items

Standard Quota:
SQ = subgroup pop / SD

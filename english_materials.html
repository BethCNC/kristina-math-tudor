üí° Writing Tips for Success

  • Start early: Begin working on essays at least one week before the due date to allow time for revision.
  • Thesis first: Craft a clear, specific thesis statement before writing your first draft.
  • Cite as you go: Add citations while drafting to avoid losing track of sources.
  • Read aloud: Reading your essay out loud helps catch awkward phrasing and errors.
  • Peer review: Have someone else read your draft and provide feedback.
  • Revision vs. editing: Revise for content and structure first, then edit for grammar and mechanics.

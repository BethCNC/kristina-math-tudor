Basic Probability

Probability Formula

P(event) = favorable outcomes ÷ total outcomes

P(A) = n(A)/n(S)

Complement Rule

P(not A) = 1 - P(A)

P(A') = 1 - P(A)

Addition Rule

P(A or B) = P(A) + P(B) - P(A and B)

P(A∪B) = P(A) + P(B) - P(A∩B)

Conditional Probability

Conditional Probability

P(A|B) = P(A and B) ÷ P(B)

P(A|B) = P(A∩B)/P(B)

Multiplication Rule

P(A and B) = P(A) × P(B|A)

P(A∩B) = P(A) × P(B|A)

Expected Value

MAT 143 Unit 4 Formula Sheet - Statistics

Z-Score Formula

z = (data - mean) / standard deviation

For Populations:

z = (x - μ) / σ - x = data value - μ = population mean - σ = population standard deviation

For Samples:

z = (x - x̄) / s - x = data value - x̄ = sample mean - s = sample standard deviation

Empirical Rule (68-95-99.7 Rule)

For normally distributed data: - 68% of data falls within 1 standard deviation of the mean - 95% of data falls within 2 standard deviations of the mean - 99.7% of data falls within 3 standard deviations of the mean

Probability

P(event) = favorable outcomes / total outcomes

Expected Value: E(X) = Σ(x × P(x))

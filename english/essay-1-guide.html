Assignment Overview

The Narrative Essay asked you to tell a significant personal story that demonstrates growth, learning, or insight.

Length: 750-1000 words
Format: MLA 9th Edition
Focus: Personal experience

Key Elements of a Narrative Essay

  • • Engaging opening: Hook readers with a compelling first sentence
  • • Clear narrative: Tell your story in chronological order (or with purposeful flashbacks)
  • • Vivid details: Use sensory language and specific descriptions
  • • Dialogue (optional): Include conversations to bring scenes to life
  • • Reflection: Explain what you learned or how you changed

Content Requirements

Format Requirements

1. Research

Find credible sources

Sources:

  • Academic databases
  • Peer-reviewed journals
  • Books and e-books
  • Government sources

2. Analysis

Analyze and synthesize

Process:

  • Read and annotate
  • Identify key themes
  • Find connections
  • Develop argument

3. Drafting

Write research paper

Structure:

  • Introduction
  • Literature review
  • Analysis sections
  • Conclusion

4. Revision

Revise and polish

Check:

  • MLA formatting
  • Word count (1500-2000)
  • Citations accuracy
  • Final proofread

Detailed Rubric

Focus & Thesis (25 points)

Criteria Excellent (23-25) Good (20-22) Needs Work (0-19)
Thesis Clear, specific, arguable thesis that presents sophisticated analysis Thesis present but could be more specific or analytical Weak, vague, or missing thesis

Organization (25 points)

Criteria Excellent (23-25) Good (20-22) Needs Work (0-19)
Structure Logical progression, clear topic sentences, smooth transitions Generally organized but some transitions could be stronger Confusing organization, unclear connections between ideas

Development & Evidence (25 points)

Criteria Excellent (23-25) Good (20-22) Needs Work (0-19)
Analysis & Evidence Thorough analysis with strong evidence from sources; goes beyond summary Adequate evidence but analysis could be deeper Insufficient evidence or mostly summary rather than analysis

Style (15 points)

Criteria Excellent (14-15) Good (12-13) Needs Work (0-11)
Voice & Clarity Engaging academic voice, varied sentence structure, clear word choice Appropriate academic tone with some awkward phrasing Unclear writing, inappropriate tone, or repetitive sentences

Mechanics & MLA Format (10 points)

Criteria Excellent (9-10) Good (7-8) Needs Work (0-6)
Grammar & Citations Few/no errors; MLA format perfect; all sources cited correctly Minor errors; MLA mostly correct; citations generally accurate Frequent errors; MLA format issues; missing or incorrect citations

What Your Instructor Is Looking For

✅ Excellent Analysis (90-100)
  • • Sophisticated, specific thesis
  • • Deep analysis that reveals new insights
  • • Strong evidence integrated seamlessly
  • • Perfect MLA citations
  • • Engaging, clear writing
✅ Good Analysis (80-89)
  • • Clear thesis with analytical claim
  • • Solid analysis with good evidence
  • • Proper organization and flow
  • • MLA mostly correct
  • • Few grammatical errors
⚠️ Needs Improvement (70-79)
  • • Thesis could be more specific
  • • More summary than analysis
  • • Some organizational issues
  • • Citation errors present
  • • Some mechanical errors

Works Cited Guide

MLA 9th Edition Works Cited examples and formatting

Works Cited Page Format

  • Start on a new page after your essay
  • Center the title "Works Cited" at top (no bold, italics, or underline)
  • Double-space all entries
  • Use hanging indent (first line flush left, rest indented Â½ inch)
  • Alphabetize by author's last name (or by title if no author)

Common Citation Examples

Book (Print)

Author Last, First. Book Title. Publisher, Year.

Website

Author Last, First. "Article Title." Website Name, Publisher, Date, URL.

Journal Article

Author Last, First. "Article Title." Journal Name, vol. X, no. X, Year, pages.

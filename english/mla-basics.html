MLA Formatting Basics

MLA 9th Edition formatting requirements for ENG 111 essays

Page Setup & Formatting

General Format

  • Font: 12-point Times New Roman
  • Double-spaced throughout
  • 1-inch margins on all sides
  • Indent first line of each paragraph ½ inch

Header & Page Numbers

  • Header: Last name + page number
  • Place in upper right corner
  • ½ inch from top of page
  • Number every page (including first)

First Page Heading

In the upper left corner of the first page, include:

Your Name Instructor Name Course Name and Number Date (Day Month Year) Essay Title (Centered)

Example: Kristina Cartwright
Professor Smith
ENG 111-N815
15 November 2025

Title Format

  • Center your title (do NOT bold, underline, or italicize)
  • Use standard capitalization (capitalize first word and all major words)
  • Place title on the line after the date
  • Begin your essay text on the next line (double-spaced from title)

Works Cited Page

  • Start Works Cited on a new page
  • Center the title "Works Cited" (no bold, italics, or underline)
  • Double-space all entries
  • Use hanging indent (first line flush left, subsequent lines indented ½ inch)
  • Alphabetize entries by author's last name (or by title if no author)

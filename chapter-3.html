Working Backwards

Sometimes it's easier to start from the end and work backwards!

Example: Magic Number

I'm thinking of a number. I multiply it by 3, then add 7, and get 22. What's my number?

Start with: 22

Subtract 7: 22 - 7 = 15

Divide by 3: 15 √∑ 3 = 5

The mystery number is 5!

Study Guide & Practice Tips

Key Takeaways

  • 2. Choose the right tool for the job - different problems need different strategies
  • 3. Show your work step by step - it helps you and helps you find mistakes
  • 4. Always check your answer - does it make sense in the real world?
